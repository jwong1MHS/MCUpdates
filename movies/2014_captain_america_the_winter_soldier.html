Captain America: The Winter Soldier

Released on: April 4, 2014
Total Gross: $714.4 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 92%
After working for S.H.I.E.L.D. for a number of months, Steve begins to realize that there is corruption within the organization. Teaming up with Natasha, Fury, and new ally Sam Wilson, they must fight against the threat within their own organization that threatens the lives of countless innocents.
