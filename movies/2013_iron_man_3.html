Iron Man 3

Released on: May 3, 2013
Total Gross: $1.215 billion
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 78%
As Tony attempts to recover from the trauma experienced during the battle of New York just months prior, a terrorist known as the Mandarin claims responsibility for a series of bombings. Tony challenges the Mandarin to come fight him, but is woefully unprepared when the fight actually comes to him.
