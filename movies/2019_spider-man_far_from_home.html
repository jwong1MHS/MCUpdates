Spider-Man: Far From Home

Released on: July 2, 2019
Total Gross: $1.132 billion

Weighted by the responsibility of becoming the next Iron Man in Tony's absence, Peter hopes to simply be a regular high school student on his field trip to Europe. However, when monsters from another dimension called Elementals start showing up, Fury and superhero Quentin Beck, who also hails from the other universe, demand that Peter puts his vacation on hold to stop this new threat.
