Thor: The Dark World

Released on: November 8, 2013
Total Gross: $644.8 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 76%
When Thor's friend Jane Foster is infected by a mysterious substance known as the Aether, Thor must enlist help from Loki to protect her from the Dark Elf Malekith, who will stop at nothing to use the Aether to attack all Nine Realms.
