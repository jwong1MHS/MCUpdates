Guardians of the Galaxy

Released on: August 1, 2014
Total Gross: $772.8 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 92%
In the deep reaches of space, a thief named Peter Quill attempts to steal a mysterious orb to sell for a high price, but is unaware of its immense power and the threat that it could bring to the galaxy. Teaming up with a ragtag group of criminals, including the assassin Gamora, the bounty hunters Rocket and Groot, and the nigh-psychopathic Drax the Destroyer, it is up to Quill and his new acquaintances to save the galaxy from the Kree that wish to gain the orb's power.
