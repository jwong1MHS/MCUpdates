Avengers: Endgame

Released on: April 26, 2019
Total Gross: $2.798 billion
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 90%
Following the end of Avengers: Infinity War, the remaining heroes are at a loss as to what they should do to undo the countless deaths caused by Thanos. The reappearcance of Scott gives everyone hope to find a way to gain the Infinity Stones and return all of the lives that were taken.
