Captain Marvel

Released on: March 8, 2019
Total Gross: $1.128 billion
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 51%
During the 90s, Kree soldier Vers is fighting against the shape-shifting Skrull army, resulting in her crashing to Earth. There, she teams up with Nick Fury to not only fight against the Skrulls, but also uncover her lost past as U.S. air force pilot Carol Danvers.
