The Incredible Hulk

Released on: June 13, 2008
Total Gross: $263.4 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 70%
General Thaddeus "Thunderbolt" Ross enlists Bruce Banner to help him in an experiment to make soldiers immune to radiation, which is actually a Super Soldier experiment in disguise. When the experiment goes wrong, Bruce absorbs all of the gamma radiation; following this, he now transforms into the Hulk when his anger reaches a peak. Spending years in isolation to protect those around him, General Ross ends this peace by trying to track down Bruce and capture him.
