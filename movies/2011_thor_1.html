Thor

Released on: May 6, 2011
Total Gross: $449.3 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 76%
After his recklessness nearly sparks a war between worlds, the God of Thunder Thor is banished by his father, Odin, to Earth to learn humility. While he bonds with humans on Earth and slowly regains his worthiness to wield his own power, his brother Loki hatches a scheme to gain Odin's favor and the throne in Thor's absence.
