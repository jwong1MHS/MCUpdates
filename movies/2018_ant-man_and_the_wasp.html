Ant-Man and the Wasp

Released on: July 6, 2018
Total Gross: $622.7 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 76%
While under house arrest due to his siding with Captain America during Civil War, Scott finds himself once again pulled into Hank's schemes as he and his daughter Hope plan to use him to free his wife from the Quantum Realm. All the while, several individuals are in pursuit of Hank's technology, putting the entire mission in jeopardy.
