Ant-Man

Released on: July 17, 2015
Total Gross: $519.3 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 86%
Following his release from prison and vow to not steal again, Scott Lang is forced to steal once more when he is encouraged by an anonymous individual to steal an unknown object from scientist Hank Pym. After succeeding, he learns that Hank was the one to encourage him to steal it, and is enlisted to use his shrinking technology to steal the same kind of technology from Hank's corrupt ex-protege.
