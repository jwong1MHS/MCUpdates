Thor: Ragnarok

Released on: November 3, 2017
Total Gross: $854 million
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 87%
Upon the return of Hela, the older sister of Thor, Thor and Loki find themselves flung to the far reaches of the universe, with Thor quickly becoming enslaved as a gladiator. After discovering that the Hulk and a fellow asgardian are also on this world, Thor must team up with them and Loki in order to escape and prevent Hela from destroying Asgard.
