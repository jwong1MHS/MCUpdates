Avengers: Age of Ultron

Released on: May 1, 2015
Total Gross: $1.403 billion
Audience Score on Rotten Tomatoes: 83%
After recovering Loki's scepter, Tony attempts to research it to create an AI that can protect the world. This AI, named Ultron, turns against the humans he was supposed to protect. The Avengers must end Ultron's terror, as well as subdue the threats of Wanda and Pietro Maximoff, two enhanced humans with a vendetta against Tony that are willing to work with Ultron if it means bringing Tony down.
