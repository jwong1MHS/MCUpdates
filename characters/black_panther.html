Black Panther

Portrayed by: Chadwick Boseman
Appears in: Captain America: Civil War, Avengers: Infinity War, Black Panther, Avengers: Endgame
The son of King T'Chaka who trained for the mantle of the Black Panther. After his father was killed in a terrorist attack, he took the mantle and temporary rule of Wakanda. Now as king, he protects Wakanda's huge cache of Vibranium.
