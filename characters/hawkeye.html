Clint Barton

Portrayed by: Jeremy Renner
Appears in: Thor, The Avengers, Avengers: Age of Ultron, Captain America: Civil War, Avengers: Endgame
An agent of S.H.I.E.L.D. and outstanding marksman, not much was initially known about Clint's past. However, he in fact has a wife and two (later three) kids that live in a secluded farm to keep them out of harm's way, which is a constant presence as an agent. He was the one to recruit Natasha into S.H.I.E.L.D. after being tasked with killing her; after he spared her, the two formed an incredibly close bond. Definitely the most down to Earth member of the Avengers, Clint always keeps a level head and acts as a support for a number of members of the team, especially Wanda Maximoff.
