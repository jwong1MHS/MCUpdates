Stephen Strange(Dr. Strange)

Portrayed by: Benedict Cumberbatch
Appears in: Dr. Strange, Thor Ragnarok, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame
Dr. Stephen Strange was a brilliant neurosurgeon until he got in a horrible car accident. After this, his hand was terribly injured and unusable. Dr. Strange traveled the world and met the Ancient One. He became the Sorcerer Supreme and uses the mystic arts to defeat his opponents.
