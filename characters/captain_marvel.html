Captain Marvel

Portrayed by: Brie Larson
Appears in: Captain Marvel, Avengers: Endgame
Carol Danvers aka Captain Marvel was a pilot in the Air Force and was a member in joint project known as Pegasus. During a test flight, along with a scientist, they were attacked by an alien species known as the Kree. They crashed and the scientist was killed. Carol survived and then shot the engine in which lead to an explosion. This explosion led to Carol absorbing the energy of the space stone. With this power, Carol was nearly indestructible.
