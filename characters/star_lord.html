Star Lord

Portrayed by: Chris Pratt
Appears in: Guardians of the Galaxy, Guardians of the Galaxy 2, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame
The son of a human woman and an unknown alien, Quill was taken by the Scavenger known as Yondu into space after his mother's death to brain cancer. Several years later, Quill still acted as a thief until an incident involving the Power Stone got him arrested with Gamora, Rocket, and Groot. Following their break out, they continued working together, calling themselves the Guardians of the Galaxy, with Quill taking the role of leader and the nickname "Star Lord". While he is an experienced fighter, his lack of parental guidance has left him less than mature.
