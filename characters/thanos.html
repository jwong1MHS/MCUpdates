Thanos

Portrayed by: Josh Brolin
Appears in: The Avengers, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame
Thanos is also known as the mad titan. He is one of the most powerful being in the MCU. He is from the planet of Titan. Thanos' main goal is to collect all of the infinity stones and bring balance to the universe.
