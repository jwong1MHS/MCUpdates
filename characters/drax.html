Drax

Portrayed by: Dave Bautista
Appears in: Guardians of the Galaxy, Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame
Drax was once an ordinary, kind husband and father; that all changed when his wife and daughter were murdered by Ronan the Accuser, and since then, he has been a bloodthirsty psychopath out for vengence. Irrational and dangerous, it took teaming up with Quill and the other Guardians to get him to mellow out.
