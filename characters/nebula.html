Nebula

Portrayed by: Karen Gillan
Appears in: Guardians of the Galaxy, Guardians of the Galaxy 2, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame
Nebula is the real daughter of Thanos the Titan. Her and her sister Gamora would fight many times and when she lost she would replace that body part with cybernetic enhancements. She turned against her father and is now an Avenger.
