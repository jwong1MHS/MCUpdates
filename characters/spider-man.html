Peter Parker

Portrayed by: Tom Holland
Appears in: Captain America: Civil War, Spider-Man: Homecoming, Avengers: Infinity War, Avengers: Endgame, Spider-Man: Far From Home
A young high schooler who, after bitten by a radioactive spider, was given superhuman strength and reflexes, as well as the ability to cling to walls. After discovering this, he took on the mantle of Spider-Man. Initially working on his own, he eventually began to get assistance from Tony, gaining better technology and support. As young as he is, Peter makes countless mistakes when trying to balance his life as a hero and his life a high schooler; nonetheless, he has an unquestionably kind heart and desperately wants to do what he can to help others.
